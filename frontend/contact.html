Frequently Asked Questions

When will RoomSpot launch?

We're currently in beta testing with select universities. Join our waitlist to be notified when we launch in your area.

How do I get beta access?

Students with .edu email addresses get priority access to our beta program. Sign up with your school email to join early.

Is RoomSpot free to use?

Yes! RoomSpot is completely free for students. We believe affordable housing should be accessible to everyone.

How do you verify listings?

We use a combination of school verification, user reviews, and our own verification process to ensure all listings are legitimate.
